# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 4, 2026**

Tracky ("the App") is developed by Jason Xu. This privacy policy explains how the App collects, uses, and protects your information.

## Information We Collect

### Location Data
The App requests access to your device's location to show your position on the map relative to nearby train stations. Location data is used only on-device and is **never transmitted to any server**.

### Calendar Data
With your permission, the App reads your device calendar to find past Amtrak train trips and add them to your travel history. Calendar data is processed entirely on-device and is **never transmitted to any server**.

### Schedule & Real-Time Data
The App fetches publicly available Amtrak schedule data (GTFS) and real-time train position data (GTFS-RT) from third-party sources:
- **Amtrak** (content.amtrak.com) — static schedule data
- **Transitdocs** (transitdocs.com) — real-time train positions and delays
- **Open-Meteo** (open-meteo.com) — weather conditions at stations

These requests include only the data necessary to display train information (e.g., geographic coordinates for weather). No personal information is sent to these services.

### Locally Stored Data
The App stores the following data on your device using local storage:
- Saved trains and completed trip history
- User preferences (units, notification settings)
- Cached schedule data (refreshed weekly)
- Debug logs (stored locally, only shared if you choose to send a bug report)

**None of this data is transmitted to any server or third party unless you explicitly choose to send a bug report via email.**

## Data Sharing
We do **not** sell, share, or transmit your personal data to any third party. All data processing occurs on your device.

## Notifications
With your permission, the App sends local notifications to alert you about upcoming trains and delays. Notifications are scheduled entirely on-device.

## Widgets & Live Activities
The App offers home screen widgets and iOS Live Activities to display train information. This data is derived from locally cached schedules and real-time feeds. No personal data is shared with the widget system beyond what is necessary to render train information.

## Children's Privacy
The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
